About This Model

This predictor uses Logistic Regression trained on 1187 historical IPL matches (2008-2026). Features include team win rate, head-to-head record, venue, and toss details. Cross-validated accuracy is approximately 50%, reflecting the inherent unpredictability of T20 cricket — even professional analytics systems rarely exceed 65-70% accuracy.
